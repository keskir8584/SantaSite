Biographies

Pepper Mint is the strongest elf you will ever meet. He might not look like much but he can take you down in a heart beat. He is Head of Elf Security and is in charge of guarding the location of Santa's Village so no children or adults can get in. His most important job is keeping Mr. and Mrs. Claus safe.
You probably already know Dasher. He is the strongest out of all the reindeer which makes him head of the reindeer. When it isn't Christmas season Dasher still helps around the shop moving hefty things Santa can't. Don't tell the other Reindeer but Santa likes Dasher the most.
Holly Joy is the most talented elf in the workshop. All those decorations and wrapping papers were designed by her. She always has an idea for anything you give her and her finished project will blow you away. She always has an eye for detail and make sure that every Christmas is an unforgettable one.
