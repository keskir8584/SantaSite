Official Santa Site

Welcome to the Official Santa Site. This site is to help Santa and his elves with their work. This is to help make everything more organized and easier to navigate. There is a naughty or Nice list so if Santa forgets who's on the list he can easily pull it up. There is also a camera feed so Santa can make sure everyone is working and is on task.

There are also biographies of some of the greatest workers throughout the workshop. This can help Santa know who are the most hardworking and trustworthy workers in his shop.
